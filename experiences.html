Experiences

I began my career as an architect and graphic designer because of my passion for visuals. Over time, I craved a challenge, so I began to train as a UX Designer. One aspect of my job I love is that I can solve problems for a company by researching. I use my skill set to look into what the customers want and need and figure out how to make their experience better. I am interested in working as a UX designer because I want to help companies create products that are easy and enjoyable to use. I can prototype and wireframe to help you understand how people might use a product.
